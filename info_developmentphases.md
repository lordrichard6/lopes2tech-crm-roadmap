# Development Phases - Step Explanations

## Main Concept

15-month development roadmap divided into 5 phases with 30 sprints (2-week development cycles). Each phase builds on the previous one to create a complete CRM system.

**Total Timeline**: 15 months  
**Total Sprints**: 30 sprints (each sprint = 2 weeks)  
**Team Size**: 5-7 team members

---

## Phase 1: Foundation & Core Features
**Duration**: 3 months (6 sprints)

### What it does
Builds the basic foundation and essential customer management features.

### Key Objectives
- **Database design and architecture** - create the core database structure
- **User authentication & RBAC** - secure login and role-based permissions
- **Customer management module** - central customer database
- **Document management system** - store and organize files
- **Product & policy catalog** - list all insurance products
- **Policy lifecycle management** - track policies from start to renewal

### Deliverables
- Customer 360Â° view dashboard - complete customer information in one place
- Multi-location data access - all offices can access the same data
- FINMA compliance framework - meet regulatory requirements
- Audit logging system - track all system actions

### Success Criteria
- All advisors can access customer data
- Policy information accurate and up-to-date
- FINMA compliance requirements met

## Phase 2: Sales & Lead Management
**Duration**: 3 months (6 sprints)

### What it does
Adds sales pipeline and lead management capabilities to convert prospects into customers.

### Key Objectives
- **Lead capture system integration** - automatically capture leads from website and other sources
- **Lead scoring and qualification** - prioritize the best leads
- **Sales pipeline management** - track deals through the sales process
- **Opportunity tracking** - monitor potential sales
- **Quote generation engine** - create insurance quotes automatically
- **Multi-provider comparison** - compare offerings from different insurers

### Deliverables
- Website form integration - leads from website flow directly into CRM
- Dynamic quote generation - automated quote creation
- Sales forecasting tools - predict future sales
- Commission tracking system - track commissions earned

### Success Criteria
- 100% of leads captured and tracked
- Sales pipeline visibility achieved
- Quote generation time reduced by 70%
- Conversion rate improved by 20%

## Phase 3: Communication & Relationship
**Duration**: 3 months (6 sprints)

### What it does
Centralizes all customer communication and improves relationship management.

### Key Objectives
- **Email integration (Gmail, Outlook)** - sync all emails with CRM
- **SMS and WhatsApp integration** - send and receive messages
- **Task and activity management** - assign and track tasks
- **Calendar synchronization** - sync appointments
- **Customer service module** - handle customer inquiries and issues
- **Template management (multilingual)** - email and document templates in multiple languages

### Deliverables
- Communication hub dashboard - see all customer communications in one place
- Appointment scheduling system - book and manage meetings
- Ticket/case management - track customer service issues
- Customer satisfaction surveys - collect feedback

### Success Criteria
- All communications centralized
- Response time reduced by 50%
- Customer satisfaction maintained at 5.0
- Task completion rate >95%

## Phase 5: Mobile & Self-Service
**Duration**: 3 months (6 sprints)

### What it does
Enables mobile access for field work and customer self-service portal. (Note: AI features will be added in a later enhancement phase)

### Key Objectives
- **Native mobile apps (iOS/Android)** - smartphone apps for agents
- **Offline data access** - work without internet connection
- **Push notifications** - receive alerts on mobile devices
- **Mobile-optimized workflows** - streamlined processes for mobile use
- **Customer self-service portal** - customers can view policies and make requests online
- **Advanced reporting features** - enhanced analytics and dashboards

### Deliverables
- Mobile applications - iOS and Android apps
- Customer portal - self-service website for customers
- Advanced analytics dashboard - deeper business insights
- Mobile synchronization - seamless sync between devices

### Success Criteria
- Mobile app used by 70% of advisors
- Offline access working seamlessly
- Self-service reduces support by 40%
- Sales efficiency improved by 25%
